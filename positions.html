The laboratory of Dr. B. Franklin Pugh, Professor of Molecular Biology and Genetics, is seeking a laboratory technician to conduct molecular biology experiments. The Pugh lab has recently moved to Cornell, and is located in a newly built laboratory in the Biotechnology Building. The lab conducts state-of-the-art research in molecular biology and computational biology, with emphasis in eukaryotic gene regulation, transcription, epigenetics, epigenomics, tumor diagnostics, and high-throughput computer-aided analytic workflows. We are looking for undergraduates, graduate students, technicians, and postdocs who are excited and motivated to push the frontiers of gene regulation research. Please inquire with Dr. Pugh
